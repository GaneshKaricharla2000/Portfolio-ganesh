ABOUT ME

"Ever since I was a little kid, software development has been my passion. I still remember sitting in front of a computer, with many games loaded into it, making it follow my instructions (Go Right,.. No no.. Go-left) As I went through the C programming tutorial, it was almost magic, being able to get the computer to do whatever I wanted, just by typing in these funny little symbols, limited only by my own imagination an d ingenuity. Throughout middle and high school, I latched on to any ideas for projects I could get, moving from "Hello World" all the way to a simple projects like Personal Password Manager and then a step high, I learned C, then C++, tearing through any books and video lectures I could find on anything remotely related to software development. I was insatiable.

This passion carried me through my education in Computer Science and Engineering. Here there were more resources available to me than I ever could have imagined as a kid I studied new languages, algorithms, compilers, higher mathematics, all with pretty much the same fascination that drove me as a kid. And it was here that I learned truly how much there was yet to learn."
